GDPR Compliance Notice

Jobscan, in accordance with the GDPR, will ask explicit consent of our EU users before information is stored or collected. This happens in a few places:

  • Cookies - Our site stores cookies on your computer. These cookies are used to improve our website experience by remembering your preferences. To decline and erase current Jobscan cookies, click Opt Out below.
  • Guest Scans - Our site processes your resume to analyze it and offer optimization tips based on your resume content. If you don't want this information to be stored on our server, please refer to our GDPR update to request your data to be deleted.
  • Profile Data - If you sign up with a Jobscan account, our site stores your personal data. We need this data for the core functionality of Jobscan to work. We will never sell your data to third parties and will only keep the minimum data possible. If you want to permanently delete your Jobscan account, please refer to our GDPR update to request your account to be deleted.
